Our Team

A1111336

董聿欣 (劇本 / 分鏡 / 動畫與特效 / 模型製作)

Script & Animation Production
分工內容：
前期專注於劇情腳本、企劃書撰寫、分鏡繪製，並負責人物與場景設計。動畫製作階段負責人物、場景與物件的建置與運鏡；後期包辦特效、後製調整、剪接、音效及音樂剪接，並主要負責模型列印作業。
寄語：
「Light&Dust動畫見證主角們從理念分歧到和解的旅程，希望大家能喜歡我們的動畫短片。」
Responsibilities:
In the early stages, focused on scriptwriting, proposal writing, storyboard drawing, and the design of characters and scenes. During the animation stage, responsible for building and framing characters, scenes, and objects. Later, handled VFX, post-production, editing, sound, and music editing, as well as 3D model printing.
Reflection:
"Through the interplay of light and dust, witness the protagonists' journey from ideological divergence to reconciliation. We hope you enjoy our animated short film.."

A1111313

鍾昀霓 (文本企劃與網站架設 / 展示規劃)

Project Planning & Exhibition Strategy
分工內容：
負責文本編修與企劃書整合。美術前期參與場景草圖繪製，並協助場景與物件的模型製作。重心著重於動畫製作之外的事項，包括平面周邊印製、網站架設以及整體展示規劃。
寄語：
「在現代價值的衝突與碰撞中，尋找重新理解彼此的契機，希望大家能喜歡我們的故事與展出。」
Responsibilities:
Responsible for text editing and proposal integration. Participated in scene sketching during the early art phase and assisted with scene and object models. Focused mainly on non-animation tasks, including printed peripheral materials, website development, and exhibition planning.
Reflection:
"Amid the conflicts and collisions of modern values, we seek the opportunity to understand each other anew. We hope you enjoy our story and exhibition."
